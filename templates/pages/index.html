Mission Statement

Provide area college students an avenue to gain prospective students as they move from their academic environment to that first tech job coming out of college.

  • Enable students to smoothly transition into the working world and quickly maximize their personal potential.
  • Provide a resource to help them make a smooth transition into the working world and to be able to quickly grow in that job and maximize their personal and professional potential.

The program can also allow professional SIM members to develop and fine tune their mentorship skills that are a key part of developing a technology team and organization.
